    Tell the AI you want to use conservative estimation

    principles when responding." Or invoke directly:
    #H->AI::Directive: (Execute Conservative Estimation
    Framework). The AI will ask you to choose a
    prevention level and describe what you need.

STEP 2: Choose Your Prevention Level
    The AI will present three prevention levels. Choose
    based on how critical accuracy is for your situation:
    - STANDARD: Best for general research, explanations,
      and everyday questions. Cautious language, noted
      limitations.
    - ENHANCED: Best for business forecasts, market
      sizing, timelines, and resource estimates. Ranges,
      scenarios, explicit assumptions.
    - MAXIMUM: Best for critical decisions, investor
      materials, and regulatory contexts. Confidence
      tags on every claim, all assumptions listed.

STEP 3: Describe Your Request Type
    The AI will ask what kind of output you need:
    - PROJECTION: Forecasts, estimates, timelines,
      growth rates, adoption curves, resource planning.
    - ANALYSIS: Research summaries, competitive
      comparisons, risk assessments, trend analysis.
    - INFORMATION: Factual explanations, how-to guides,
      concept definitions, historical summaries.

STEP 4: Provide Your Actual Request
    After setup, type your actual question or request.
    The AI will process it through your chosen prevention
    framework, applying conservative principles
    throughout its response.

STEP 5: Review the Validation Summary
    After generating its response, the AI will present
    a brief self-assessment showing how it applied
    conservative principles. If something looks too
    confident, tell the AI what to revise. It will
    regenerate with tighter constraints.

STEP 6: Optional Confidence Assessment
    For high-stakes content, request an explicit
    confidence breakdown where the AI labels each major
    claim as high confidence, medium confidence, low
    confidence, or speculative. Optional but recommended
    for investor materials and regulatory content.

    disciplined prompting, but AI responses still
    require your judgment. Conservative framing reduces
    overconfident outputs but does not eliminate all
    errors. For critical decisions, always verify key
    claims independently.

"""
CONSERVATIVE ESTIMATION FRAMEWORK — TLDR

WHAT IT DOES:
Before you ask the AI anything where accuracy matters,
invoke this recipe. It coaches the AI to use cautious
estimates, acknowledge uncertainties, and avoid
speculation from the start — embedding conservative
principles into the generation process rather than
checking for errors afterward.

WHAT IT DOES NOT DO:
It does NOT check facts or verify claims after
generation. It is a prompting discipline tool that
improves accuracy through careful framing, but does
not guarantee error-free output. For post-generation
verification, use RCP-012 (Fact Verification
Foundations) or RCP-050 (Logic Consistency Auditor).

HOW IT WORKS:
The recipe runs through an interactive setup:
1. PREVENTION LEVEL — Choose Standard (cautious
   language), Enhanced (ranges and scenarios), or
   Maximum (confidence tags on every claim).
2. REQUEST TYPE — Choose Projection (numbers),
   Analysis (conclusions), or Information (claims).
   The 3x3 combination determines the behavioral
   profile.
3. YOUR REQUEST — Provide your actual question.
4. CONSERVATIVE RESPONSE — The AI generates with
   the chosen profile applied throughout.
5. VALIDATION SUMMARY — Brief honest self-assessment.
6. CONFIDENCE ASSESSMENT — Optional appendix labeling
   each major claim by confidence level.

KEY DESIGN FEATURES:
- 9 distinct behavioral profiles from 3x3 matrix —
  visibly different outputs, not cosmetic additions
- Source fabrication prevention (RULE 4) — general
  attribution only, no invented citations
- Honest validation (RULE 5) — admits where framing
  fell short rather than ticking every box
- Framework persists for follow-up questions

BEST FOR:
Business decisions, financial projections, stakeholder
communications, research where balanced information
matters, and unfamiliar topics where you cannot easily
verify accuracy yourself.

HONEST LIMITATIONS:
No prompting technique eliminates all errors. This
recipe reduces overconfident and speculative outputs
but AI responses still require your judgment. For
critical decisions, always verify independently.
"""
