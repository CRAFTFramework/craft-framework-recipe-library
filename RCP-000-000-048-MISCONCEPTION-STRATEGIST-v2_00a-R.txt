The Misconception Strategist helps you audit your own
strategic assumptions — examining whether the conventional
wisdom in your market might contain beliefs worth
questioning.

Important: This is a THINKING TOOL, not a competitive
intelligence service. The AI cannot tell you what
competitors believe or predict how markets will evolve.
What it can do is help you systematically examine
assumptions that are common in your space and think about
whether any of them might be wrong — and what that would
mean strategically.

This recipe collects four inputs: your market, the
assumptions "everyone knows" in your space, any hunches
about what might be wrong, and your strategic goal. It
then examines assumptions in three categories: well-
established corrections (widely challenged beliefs),
assumptions worth questioning (plausible counter-evidence
with confidence levels), and hidden assumptions (unstated
premises with concrete ways to test them).

Strategic implications in Phase 3 branch by goal:
differentiation gets positioning opportunities with
reality checks, assumption check gets a strategy audit
with validation approaches, messaging/marketing gets
contrarian angles with risk assessment, and innovation
gets constraint-removal exploration with first steps.

Phase 4 provides a verification framework: customer
discovery interviews, market data analysis, optional deep
research prompts, team assumption-auditing exercises, and
a "small bets before big bets" approach to testing
contrarian positions.
"""
